Inspiration

Fashion today has become almost an obligation. We feel compelled to continually change our appearance to keep up with worthless passenger trends. The fashion system legitimizes the uncontrolled accumulation of clothes that we wear too little, wash too often and throw away too quickly. Project Kire was born as a reaction to wastefulness and unbridled consumption.
